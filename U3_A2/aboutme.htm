About Me

My journey at Coleg Cambria and BTEC IT began in September 2022. On a good occasion, I joined the 'Entry to ICT' course. There, I met an amazing team of tutors who introduced me to the world of technology. Before that, I wasn’t very interested in computing or technology, but I quickly became impressed. I was amazed by what technology could do for people and what people could create with it. I was proud to have the highest attendance of 2023 – Coleg Cambria. A year later, I completed my BTEC Level 1 ICT course. It wasn’t easy, but with hard work I achieved an expected merit grade and now I am finishing level 2 BTEC, and I am proud of my accomplishments .
