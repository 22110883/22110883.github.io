Unit 1

In this unit, I learned how the internet works and how I used it in my daily life. I explored how websites appeared on my screen, how emails reached my computer, and how different apps and online tools helped me stay connected and complete tasks. I looked at devices like smartphones and music players, and I understood how they shared information. I also learned about new technologies, like smart appliances and wearable devices, that connect to the internet and make life easier. I studied how to stay safe online and how to protect myself while respecting others. This unit helped me build useful skills, especially for a future career in IT or to better understand the digital world around me.
