Unit 13

I visited many websites and often wondered how they were made. I learned that websites can include different things like text, pictures, animations, videos, and programs. Some websites also had features like search bars, online shopping, and messaging. To work well, a website needed to look good and be easy to use.

I found out that websites are very important today. People and businesses use them to talk, share information, and sell products. Because of this, there are many jobs for people who know how to make and manage websites. For example:

  • Web developer – builds websites
  • Content manager – keeps the website up to date
  • SEO specialist – helps websites appear in search engine results

In this unit, I learned how websites work and how all the different parts come together. I designed, built, and tested my own website using a given brief. After that, I got feedback from others and used it to make my website better.

This unit helped me use skills I learned in:

  • Unit 1: The Online World
  • Unit 2: Technology Systems
  • Unit 4: Creating Digital Animation
  • Unit 5: Creating Digital Audio
  • Unit 6: Creating Digital Graphics
  • Unit 7: Creating Digital Video
  • Unit 12: Software Development
